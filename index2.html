about

I'm motivated to build products that level the field, using technology to help people find opportunities, information and experiences that they wouldn't have access otherwise.

I try to focus my bets in these 3 beliefs:
- Universal access: every person in the planet will have access to the internet and use it on a daily basis.
- Interface covergence: Mobile technology will continue to replace, one by one, every other way of connecting us with people, content and services.
- Smart, for real: in the next few years, machine learning will take care of a lot of the mental heavy-lifting from users, making today's softwares look dumb.

On my free time, I'm either outdoors or enjoying a cup of coffee while doing some sort of creative project.
